Welcome to my web site! I am an academic member of Information Systems Group in the School of Industrial Engineering at Eindhoven University of Technology. My research interests are software measurement&estimation, requirements engineering, Software Product & Process Quality and Improvement, Business Process Management, IT & SW Governance, Risk and Sustainable Software Engineering.

I am also interested in software development technologies and I like exploring new architectures, development technologies and frameworks(in particular enterprise development with FOSS frameworks and libraries).

In my free time I listen to music (check my Music page), watch TV series(my favs:The Wire, Breaking Bad, Boardwalk Empire) and follow radio shows (my favorite:Cabin Pressure!)
